Adding a Worker

Workers connect to this controller over HTTP. On the remote machine, run:

curl -X POST http://YOUR_CONTROLLER_IP:8888/api/cluster/workers \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "name": "my-gaming-pc",
    "url": "http://WORKER_IP:PORT",
    "platform": "linux",
    "capabilities": ["chat", "embed", "image-generation"],
    "hardware": {"cpu": "Ryzen 9", "ram_gb": 64, "gpu": "RTX 4090"},
    "models": ["llama3-70b"]
  }'

The worker should send periodic heartbeats to POST /api/cluster/heartbeat with its name and current load. Workers that miss heartbeats for 30 seconds are marked offline.
